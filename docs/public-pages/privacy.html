Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your phone

Debt Freedom is built so that none of your financial information ever leaves your device. This page explains exactly how that works.

Short version

Debt Freedom does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal or financial data. Everything you enter is stored privately on this device.

What information the app handles

When you use Debt Freedom you enter information about your finances — the names and balances of your debts, interest rates, your monthly budget, payday, savings goals, and which months you've ticked off as complete.

This information is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage, which uses iOS's secure local storage under the hood). It is never uploaded to any server, never sent over the internet, and never shared with anyone — including us.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or other identifiers.
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, advertising IDs, etc.).
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that collect data on our behalf.
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking on this support site beyond what GitHub Pages serves to deliver the page itself.

Where your data lives

All your data lives on your iPhone, inside the Debt Freedom app's private storage area. iOS prevents other apps from reading this area. If you delete Debt Freedom, iOS automatically removes all the data with it.

If you sign in to iCloud and have iCloud Backup turned on for Debt Freedom (Settings → your name → iCloud → iCloud Backup), Apple may include the app's local storage in your encrypted iCloud backup. That backup belongs to you and is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours.

Future cloud features

We're planning to add optional cloud sync, payment reminders, and shared plans for partners in a later update. Those features are not in the current version. If and when they ship, they will be strictly opt-in, this policy will be updated, and we will clearly explain what data leaves your device, what is stored, and for how long.

Children

Debt Freedom is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly accept any information from children.

Your rights

Because we don't hold any of your data, there is nothing for us to give you, correct, or delete on our side. You can review or delete everything you've entered at any time from within the app: open Settings → Reset everything. To remove all data instantly, delete the app from your iPhone.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Debt Freedom handles data — for example, when cloud sync launches — we will update this page and bump the "last updated" date below. Material changes will also be surfaced inside the app.
